## 1. Lightning Multiplication

**Q1.** 97 × 96 = ?
> **Answer: 9312** — Nikhilam: 97(−3), 96(−4). Left: 97−4=93. Right: 3×4=12. → 9312

**Q2.** 103 × 107 = ?
> **Answer: 11021** — Both above 100: Left: 103+7=110. Right: 3×7=21. → 11021

**Q3.** 88 × 93 = ?
> **Answer: 8184** — 88(−12), 93(−7). Left: 88−7=81. Right: 12×7=84. → 8184

**Q4.** 998 × 994 = ?
> **Answer: 992012** — Base 1000: 998(−2), 994(−6). Left: 998−6=992. Right: 012. → 992012

**Q5.** 112 × 104 = ?
> **Answer: 11648** — Left: 112+4=116. Right: 12×4=48. → 11648

**Q6.** 23 × 14 = ? (Urdhva Tiryagbhyam)
> **Answer: 322** — Step 1: 3×4=12 (write 2, carry 1). Step 2: 2×4+3×1=11+1=12 (write 2, carry 1). Step 3: 2×1+1=3. → 322

**Q7.** 45 × 67 = ?
> **Answer: 3015** — Step 1: 5×7=35(5,c3). Step 2: 4×7+5×6=58+3=61(1,c6). Step 3: 4×6+6=30. → 3015

**Q8.** 35 × 11 = ?
> **Answer: 385** — Split & sum: 3_(3+5)_5 = 385

**Q9.** 47 × 11 = ?
> **Answer: 517** — 4_(4+7)_7 = 4_(11)_7 = 517 (carry the 1)

**Q10.** 248 × 5 = ?
> **Answer: 1240** — ×5 = ×10÷2 → 2480÷2 = 1240

**Q11.** 48 × 25 = ?
> **Answer: 1200** — ×25 = ×100÷4 → 4800÷4 = 1200

**Q12.** 76 × 99 = ?
> **Answer: 7524** — Left: 76−1=75. Right: 100−76=24. → 7524

**Q13.** 432 × 999 = ?
> **Answer: 431568** — Left: 431. Right: 999−431=568. → 431568

## 2. Fast Squaring

**Q14.** 25² = ?
> **Answer: 625** — Ends in 5: 2×3=6, append 25 → 625

**Q15.** 75² = ?
> **Answer: 5625** — 7×8=56, append 25 → 5625

**Q16.** 105² = ?
> **Answer: 11025** — 10×11=110, append 25 → 11025

**Q17.** 97² = ?
> **Answer: 9409** — Near 100: (97−3)×100 + 9 = 9400+9 = 9409

**Q18.** 102² = ?
> **Answer: 10404** — (102+2)×100 + 4 = 10400+4 = 10404

**Q19.** 52² = ?
> **Answer: 2704** — Near 50: (25+2)×100 + 4 = 2704

**Q20.** 48² = ?
> **Answer: 2304** — Near 50: (25−2)×100 + 4 = 2304

**Q21.** 67² = ?
> **Answer: 4489** — Duplex: 6²|2×6×7|7² = 36|84|49. Process carries: 4489

**Q22.** 83² − 17² = ?
> **Answer: 6600** — a²−b² = (a+b)(a−b) = 100×66 = 6600

**Q23.** 56² − 44² = ?
> **Answer: 1200** — (56+44)(56−44) = 100×12 = 1200

## 4. Percentage Speed Hacks

**Q29.** 15% of 360 = ?
> **Answer: 54** — 10% = 36. 5% = 18. Total = 54

**Q30.** 12.5% of 480 = ?
> **Answer: 60** — 12.5% = 1/8. 480÷8 = 60

**Q31.** 17% of 50 = ?
> **Answer: 8.5** — Trick: 17% of 50 = 50% of 17 = 8.5

**Q32.** 4% of 75 = ?
> **Answer: 3** — Trick: 4% of 75 = 75% of 4 = 3

**Q33.** 33.33% of 450 = ?
> **Answer: 150** — 33.33% = 1/3. 450÷3 = 150

**Q34.** Price increased by 25%, then decreased by 20%. Net change?
> **Answer: 0% (no change)** — 25−20+(25×−20)/100 = 5−5 = 0%

**Q35.** Price decreased by 10% then increased by 10%. Net change?
> **Answer: 1% decrease** — (−10)+10+(−10×10)/100 = −1%

## 5. Divisibility & Remainders

**Q36.** Is 7854 divisible by 11?
> **Answer: No**
> (7+5) − (8+4) = 12−12 = 0 → **Yes, divisible by 11!** (Correction: it IS divisible)

**Q37.** Find remainder when 5834 is divided by 9.
> **Answer: 2** — Digit sum: 5+8+3+4 = 20 → 2+0 = 2

**Q38.** Find unit digit of 3^75.
> **Answer: 7** — Cycle of 3: {3,9,7,1}, period 4. 75 mod 4 = 3. Third in cycle = 7

**Q39.** Find unit digit of 8^42.
> **Answer: 4** — Cycle of 8: {8,4,2,6}, period 4. 42 mod 4 = 2. Second = 4

**Q40.** Is 1296 a perfect square?
> **Answer: Yes, 36²** — 1296: digit sum = 18→9 (valid). √1296: Try 36: 36×36 = 1296 ✓

**Q41.** Number of trailing zeros in 50!
> **Answer: 12** — ⌊50/5⌋ + ⌊50/25⌋ = 10 + 2 = 12

**Q42.** Find 2^10.
> **Answer: 1024** — Memorize: 2^10 = 1024

## 6. Digital Root Verification

**Q43.** Verify: 267 × 139 = 37113 (using digital root)
> DR(267) = 2+6+7 = 15 → 6
> DR(139) = 1+3+9 = 13 → 4
> Product of DRs = 6×4 = 24 → 6
> DR(37113) = 3+7+1+1+3 = 15 → 6
> **Both are 6 → LIKELY CORRECT ✓**

**Q44.** Is 456 × 123 = 56008 correct? (Quick check)
> DR(456) = 4+5+6 = 15 → 6
> DR(123) = 1+2+3 = 6
> Product = 6×6 = 36 → 9
> DR(56008) = 5+6+0+0+8 = 19 → 10 → 1
> **9 ≠ 1 → DEFINITELY WRONG ✗**
> (Actual: 456 × 123 = 56088)

## 7. Mixed Speed Questions (Exam Style)

**Q45.** 1000 − 748 = ? (Nikhilam subtraction)
> **Answer: 252** — All from 9, last from 10: 9−7=2, 9−4=5, 10−8=2 → 252

**Q46.** 10000 − 6237 = ?
> **Answer: 3763** — 9−6=3, 9−2=7, 9−3=6, 10−7=3 → 3763

**Q47.** 735 ÷ 5 = ?
> **Answer: 147** — ÷5 = ×2÷10 → 1470÷10 = 147

**Q48.** 625 ÷ 25 = ?
> **Answer: 25** — ÷25 = ×4÷100 → 2500÷100 = 25

**Q49.** Average speed if half distance at 40 km/hr and half at 60 km/hr?
> **Answer: 48 km/hr** — 2×40×60/(40+60) = 4800/100 = 48

**Q50.** What is √200 approximately?
> **Answer: ≈ 14.14** — Nearest square: 196=14². √200 ≈ 14 + 4/28 ≈ 14.14

**Q51.** 37² = ? (using (a×10+b)² formula)
> **Answer: 1369** — 3²×100 + 2×3×7×10 + 7² = 900+420+49 = 1369

**Q52.** If a train travels 45 km in 30 minutes, what is speed in m/s?
> **Answer: 25 m/s** — Speed = 45/0.5 = 90 km/hr = 90×5/18 = 25 m/s

**Q53.** A product costs ₹800. After 20% discount and then 10% discount, final price?
> **Answer: ₹576** — 800×0.8×0.9 = 800×0.72 = ₹576

**Q54.** Compound interest on ₹10,000 at 10% for 2 years?
> **Answer: ₹2100** — A = 10000×(1.1)² = 12100. CI = 2100

**Q55.** How many factors does 720 have?
> **Answer: 30** — 720 = 2⁴×3²×5¹. Factors = 5×3×2 = 30
