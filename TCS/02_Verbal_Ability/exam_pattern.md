## 📝 Section-wise Instructions

### 1. Sentence Completion

- Please type **one word** that best fits the meaning of the sentence.
- Type **only one word**. You will have **25 seconds** for each sentence.
- Click **"Next"** when you are finished.

#### ⏱ Time Strategy
```
Total Questions : 20
Time per Question: 25 seconds
Total Time       : ~8 minutes 20 seconds

⚡ Tips:
  → Read the sentence quickly and identify the context
  → Look for clue words (however, although, because, etc.)
  → Type the MOST common word that fits — don't overthink
  → If stuck, move on — don't waste the full 25 seconds
```

#### 📌 What to Expect
- A sentence with **one blank** will appear on screen
- You need to **type** the answer (not select from options)
- Only **one word** is accepted — no phrases
- The timer auto-advances after 25 seconds

#### 🎯 Common Word Types Asked
| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| Prepositions | in, on, at, by, for, with, to, from |
| Conjunctions | although, however, because, therefore |
| Verbs | improve, maintain, achieve, establish |
| Adjectives | significant, essential, crucial, effective |
| Articles | a, an, the |

### 2. Passage Recall

> ⚠️ **This is NOT a Reading Comprehension section. You must REWRITE the passage from memory.**

#### How It Works (Step by Step)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  STEP 1: READ (30 seconds)                              │
│  → A paragraph appears on screen                        │
│  → Read and UNDERSTAND the key ideas                    │
│  → Focus on: main topic, key facts, sequence of ideas   │
│  → DO NOT try to memorize word-for-word                 │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  STEP 2: PASSAGE DISAPPEARS                             │
│  → After 30 seconds, the text is removed from screen    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  STEP 3: WRITE (90 seconds)                             │
│  → Reconstruct the passage in YOUR OWN WORDS            │
│  → Show that you understood the content                 │
│  → Scored on CLEAR and ACCURATE content                 │
│  → NOT scored on word-for-word memorization             │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  STEP 4: AUTO-SAVE                                      │
│  → After 90 seconds, your work is saved automatically   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

#### 📊 Passage Recall Breakdown
| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Number of Passages | 4 + 4 (8 total) |
| Reading Time | 30 seconds per passage |
| Writing Time | 90 seconds per passage |
| Total Time per Passage | 120 seconds (2 minutes) |
| Total Section Time | ~16 minutes |

#### ✅ Scoring Criteria
Your answer is scored on:
1. **Content Accuracy** — Did you capture the main ideas correctly?
2. **Clarity of Expression** — Is your rewrite clear and understandable?
3. **Coverage** — Did you cover all key points from the passage?
4. **Grammar & Coherence** — Is your writing grammatically correct and well-structured?

> 🔴 **NOT scored on**: Word-for-word memorization. You need to read the passage and **recreate it in your own sentences**.

#### 🧠 Memory Strategy (The TSKD Method)

```
While reading (30 seconds), quickly note in your mind:

T — Topic: What is the passage about?
S — Structure: How many main points are there?
K — Keywords: What are the important terms/names/numbers?
D — Direction: What is the conclusion or final point?

While writing (90 seconds):
1. Start with the topic sentence (what the passage is about)
2. Write the main points in order
3. Include any specific data/names you remember
4. End with the conclusion/final statement
5. Use your own words — don't struggle to recall exact phrases
```

#### 📝 Practice Template
```
The passage discusses [TOPIC]. It explains that [MAIN POINT 1].
Furthermore, [MAIN POINT 2]. The passage also mentions that
[MAIN POINT 3]. In conclusion, [FINAL IDEA/CONCLUSION].
```

### 3. Email Writing

- Read a description of a situation and write an email addressing the issues described.
- You will have **9 minutes** (540 seconds).
- You must write **between 50 to 100 words** for the email.
- Use formal language and proper email structure.

#### ⏱ Time Strategy
```
Total Time: 9 minutes (540 seconds)

Recommended Split:
  → 1 minute  : Read the situation carefully
  → 1 minute  : Plan your email structure
  → 5 minutes : Write the email
  → 2 minutes : Review grammar, spelling, word count
```

#### 📧 Email Format Template
```
Subject: [Clear, specific subject line]

Dear [Mr./Ms./Sir/Madam/Name],

[Opening line — Purpose of the email]

[Body — Address the situation/issues (2-3 sentences)]

[Closing — Request action or provide resolution]

Regards,
[Your Name]
```

#### ✅ Scoring Criteria
| Parameter | What They Check |
|-----------|----------------|
| Relevance | Does the email address the given situation? |
| Structure | Proper email format (greeting, body, closing)? |
| Grammar | Correct grammar, spelling, and punctuation? |
| Word Count | Between 50-100 words? |
| Tone | Formal and professional? |
| Clarity | Is the message clear and concise? |

#### 🎯 Common Email Scenarios in TCS NQT
1. **Complaint email** — Product defect, poor service, delayed delivery
2. **Request email** — Leave request, schedule change, information request
3. **Apology email** — Missed deadline, error in work, absent from meeting
4. **Follow-up email** — After a meeting, pending task, awaiting response
5. **Suggestion email** — Process improvement, team activity, policy change

#### 📝 Sample Practice

**Situation:** You ordered a laptop online. It arrived damaged with a cracked screen. Write an email to customer support asking for a replacement.

```
Subject: Request for Replacement of Damaged Laptop — Order #12345

Dear Customer Support Team,

I am writing to report that the laptop I ordered (Order #12345),
delivered on 20th June, arrived with a cracked screen. The outer
packaging showed no visible damage, suggesting the product was
damaged before shipping.

I request an immediate replacement or a full refund. I have
attached photographs of the damaged product for your reference.
Please arrange a pickup for the defective item at your earliest
convenience.

Looking forward to your prompt response.

Regards,
Dilip
```
